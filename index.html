Our Donation Plan

The strategies we will undertake to make our plans successful are projected as follows.


Food for a family

Essential food items like rice, lentils vegetables, oil and sugar will be given to each family. Along with these, dry foods, cake, biscuits and tea will be given to the families to fight hunger.

Shelter for a family

Continuous waves of flood have destroyed many houses in the affected area leaving behind homeless and helpless people. We will utilize a handsome amount of our collection to build some shelter for the affected people and help them start their lives over.

education for a family

Education in another massively affected matter that we want to prioritize on. We plan to provide the young children with quality eduation that they so direly need to build a bright future for them. Only then these children can be the great leaders for the nation tomorrow.

Their lives depend on your contribution

Tackling a natural disaster is a long time process because it takes a lot from the affected victims. They become helpless as their crops, livestock's, homes and everything gets affected. To help these people recover in long run, we look forward to your valuable support.
